## Overview

Hiro's Hotel CRM module extends the core CRM with hotel-specific features designed for concierge teams, hotel managers, and multi-property groups. It manages hotel partnerships, guest referrals, and coordination between hotels and restaurants.

<Note>
**Perfect for:** Hotel groups referring guests to partner restaurants, restaurant groups working with hotel concierges, and integrated hospitality operations.
</Note>

## Guest Referral Workflow

### Concierge Referral Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Guest Requests Restaurant">
    Hotel guest asks concierge for dining recommendation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Concierge Makes Reservation">
    Concierge books table at partner restaurant:
    - Via phone or direct booking system
    - Specifies guest name and hotel
    - Notes any special requests or VIP status
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restaurant Receives Booking">
    Reservation syncs to Hiro:
    - Automatically tagged with hotel source
    - Flagged for special attention
    - Guest preferences noted
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pre-Service Preparation">
    Restaurant prepares:
    - Reviews guest profile (if returning)
    - Confirms table assignment
    - Prepares welcome amenity (if VIP)
    - Notifies staff of VIP/hotel guest
  </Step>
  <Step title="Service & Follow-up">
    During and after visit:
    - Exceptional service delivery
    - Manager table visit
    - Feedback collection
    - Thank you to concierge
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reporting Back">
    Restaurant reports to hotel:
    - Confirmation of visit
    - Guest satisfaction
    - Any issues or highlights
    - Revenue attribution
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Special Handling for Hotel Guests

Automatic VIP treatment triggers:

- **Table Assignment**: Best available table, preferably window/terrace
- **Welcome Amenity**: Complimentary amuse-bouche or aperitif
- **Service Priority**: Expedited seating, attentive service
- **Manager Visit**: Table visit to ensure satisfaction
- **Feedback Request**: Post-meal survey (high priority)

<Note type="tip">
**Pro Tip:** Train staff to ask "Are you staying at [Hotel Name]?" to catch referrals not explicitly noted by concierge.
</Note>

## Analytics & Reporting

### Hotel Performance Dashboard

Key metrics per hotel:

- **Referral Volume**: Total reservations this month/year
- **Guest Count**: Total pax referred
- **Revenue Attribution**: Total spend from hotel guests
- **Average Party Size**: Mean pax per reservation
- **Conversion Rate**: Reservations / Total guests at hotel
- **Guest Satisfaction**: Average NPS from hotel guests

- Exclude no-shows and cancellations

## Operational Workflows

### New Hotel Partner Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Meeting">
    - Meet hotel manager and concierge team
    - Present restaurant and menu
    - Discuss partnership terms
    - Set commission structure
  </Step>
  <Step title="System Configuration">
    - Add hotel to Hiro database
    - Set classification level
    - Configure commission rate
    - Create custom tags for tracking
  </Step>
  <Step title="Training">
    - Provide reservation process training
    - Share menu and wine list
    - Demonstrate booking system (if integrated)
    - Provide direct contact numbers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    - Send welcome package
    - Activate partnership status
    - Schedule first follow-up (30 days)
    - Begin referral tracking
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Monthly Partnership Review

1. **Generate Performance Report**
   - Referrals this month
   - Revenue attributed
   - Guest satisfaction scores
   - Any service issues

2. **Review with Hotel**
   - Share report with hotel manager
   - Discuss opportunities
   - Address any concerns
   - Plan upcoming promotions

3. **Process Commission**
   - Calculate commission owed
   - Generate invoice/statement
   - Submit for payment
   - Confirm receipt with hotel

4. **Plan Next Month**
   - Set referral goals
   - Schedule events or promotions
   - Update menus if changed
   - Confirm contact information
