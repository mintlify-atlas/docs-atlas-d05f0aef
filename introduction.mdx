Hiro CRM is an open-source, single-tenant CRM platform designed specifically for the hospitality industry in Spain. It combines customer management, reservation analytics, marketing campaigns, and AI-powered insights in a single modern web application.

## What is Hiro CRM?

Hiro CRM helps hospitality businesses manage their operations, understand their customers, and grow their revenue through data-driven marketing and personalized experiences. Whether you run a restaurant group, hotel chain, or mixed hospitality portfolio, Hiro CRM provides the tools you need to succeed.

### Built for hospitality

Unlike generic CRM solutions, Hiro CRM is purpose-built for restaurants, hotels, and hospitality groups. Every feature is designed around the unique needs of the industry:

- Customer profiles with visit history and preferences
- Integration with Spanish POS systems (CoverManager, Revo)
- Marketing campaigns via email and SMS through Brevo
- Loyalty programs with configurable tiers
- Multi-location management for restaurant groups
- Hotel guest management and operations

## Technology stack

Hiro CRM is built with modern, production-ready technologies:

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|------------|
| Frontend | Next.js 16, React 18, TypeScript |
| Styling | Tailwind CSS v4, Framer Motion |
| Database | Supabase (PostgreSQL + RLS) |
| Auth | Supabase Auth |
| AI | OpenAI GPT-4 |
| Email/SMS | Brevo |
| Background Jobs | Inngest |
| POS Integration | CoverManager, Revo POS (optional) |
| Deployment | Vercel |

## Single-tenant architecture

Hiro CRM is designed as a **single-tenant application** - one deployment per business. This gives you:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Full control over your data">
    Your customer data stays in your own Supabase project, not shared with anyone else
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete customization">
    Modify the codebase to fit your specific business needs without affecting other users
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Simple deployment">
    Deploy to Vercel with a single click, no complex infrastructure required
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Predictable costs">
    Pay only for your own resources, with no per-seat pricing or hidden fees
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
While Hiro CRM is designed as single-tenant, you can manage multiple locations and brands within a single deployment. This is perfect for restaurant groups or hospitality companies with multiple properties.
</Note>

## Use cases
